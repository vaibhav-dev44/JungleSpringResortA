About Jungle Spring Resort

Jungle Spring Resort blends adventure and nature in a dam backwater setting. Ideal for families, corporates, and schools, offering thrilling activities amidst lush greenery.

Activities

This is where the adventure begins

ZIPLINE

350+ ft long zipline has it's own adventurous feel which you cannot experience anywhere else, because it's just beside dam, giving you full view of dam backwater and surrounded by lush green mountains.

Safety: Harness and trained guides.

SKY CYCLE

Balance on air bike which keeps you up at 25ft from bottom in sky. No wonder why we call it sky cycle. It offers an adventurous feel, combined twists to cycling, combining speed & courage for an unforgettable ride above the ground.

Safety: Secure tracks and supervision.

ROCKET EJECTOR

It's time you meet, rocket Ejector at jungle spring resort. It can give you wings, but it can catapult you 80 feet off the ground! Rocket ejector is perfect activity for adrenaline junkies.

Safety: Controlled ejection and safety nets.

HUMAN GYRO

Get ready to challenge gravity and enjoy this amazing game !!! Where you can enjoy with your best buddies.

Safety: Stabilized platform and harnesses.

ROPE COURSE – LEVEL 1

Zigzag suspension plank Burma bridge block walk swinging plank Balancing Rope tyre bridge high V swinging ladder net tunnel

Safety: Full harness and instructor guidance.

ROPE COURSE – LEVEL 2

Up & down swinging pipe sideways tyre crossing sky walk log to log wally crossing sideways plank swinging tyre crossing zigzag swinging plank commando net vertical rope crossing

Safety: Full harness and instructor guidance.

BULL RIDE

Get your innercowboy out and show your balancing skills on bull ride. Hold the rope tightly & dare to ride.

Safety: Padded area.

MELTDOWN

Melt down game is an immersive gaming experience that transcend boundaries. Continuously push the boundaries and delivering engaging narratives and unforgettable gaming moments.

Safety: Lifeguards and safe entry.

SWIMMING POOL

Relax in our refreshing pool after adventures.

Safety: Lifeguards on duty.
